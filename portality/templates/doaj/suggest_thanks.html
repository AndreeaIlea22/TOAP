Application safely received

Thank you for your re/application which has been safely received! You will receive an automated email from us when the re/application has been assigned to an editor and then another email when they start working on it. Due to the extra workload created by the ongoing re-application project, it may take up to 6 months before you get a final decision from us. We apologise in advance for the extended wait but please be assured that we are working as hard as we can with the resources we have.

We will not reply to emails requesting a status update for re/applications that are less than 6 months old. Before you contact us requesting an update, please check your spam folder to ensure that a member of our editorial team has not already contacted you. They may not be emailing from a doaj.org email address.
