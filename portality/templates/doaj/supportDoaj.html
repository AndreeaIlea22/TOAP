Membership & Donations

DOAJ Membership is recommended for those who can support the DOAJ with an annual fixed contribution. Members are listed on the DOAJ Membership page, can use their DOAJ membership status and our logo for marketing activities and will receive our newsletter.


You may also make a donation of any amount that you wish and be listed as a DOAJ Supporter.

If you publish or own an Open Access journal, you may prefer to become a Publisher Member. You will be listed on our Publisher Members page and will receive our newsletter.
