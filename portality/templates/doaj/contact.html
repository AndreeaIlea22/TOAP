  1. I want to publish my article in your journal.
    DOAJ is not a journal or a publisher; do not submit papers to us. We are a directory of open access journals.

  2. I want to index my journal in DOAJ. What do I do?
    Have a look at our standards for selection. If your journal is still suitable please fill in the application form.

  3. I have filled in an application for my journal to be in DOAJ. How long do I have to wait?
    We process applications as fast as possible. If you have not received an answer within 3 months, feel free to contact us. Before you do so, please check your Spam folder to make sure we haven't contacted you already.

  4. Please send me articles about subject X.
    We do not supply that service. Search our catalogue

  5. Please send me a list of journals on subject X.
    You can either harvest our metadata or download a .csv file.
