We welcome feedback and comments about the DOAJ web site, our service or any content you find here. If you are sending feedback about a particular journal, be assured that all communication will be treated as confidential.

Before you contact us, read this:

  1. I want to publish my article in your journal.
    DOAJ is not a journal or a publisher; do not submit papers to us. We are a directory of open access journals.

  2. I want my journal indexed in DOAJ. What do I do?
    Have a look at our Information for Publishers page. If your journal fills our criteria please fill in an application.

  3. I have filled in an application or a reapplication. How long do I have to wait?
    We process applications as fast as possible. We recently completed a large reapplication project and have a backlog of applications to process. If you have not received an answer within 6 months, contact us using the form below. Please check your Spam folder first to make sure you have no messages from us.

  4. Please send me articles about subject X.
    We do not supply that service. Use our subject browser and then contact the relevant journal.

  5. Please send me a list of journals on subject X.
    We do not supply that service. You can either use our subject browser, harvest our metadata or download a .csv file.

To contact us please send us email to feedback[at]doaj[.]org
