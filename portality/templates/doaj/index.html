Welcome to the new DOAJ!

DOAJ has migrated to a new, open source platform! Take a look around; we welcome your feedback.

You may notice that the number of journals in DOAJ has dropped from 10 027 to 9804. This is because we have fine-tuned the counting functionality. During the migration to the new platform, we identified a group of journals whose ISSNs have changed over time, e.g. because of a title change. We have introduced a 'continuation' relationship so that a previous ISSN entry is now correctly superseded by the new ISSN and the record counted once.
